Founding Head’s Welcome

Welcome by Dr. A. Mercer

It is my privilege to welcome you to our school. From the moment a child walks through our gates, our ambition for them is simple and complete: that they should flourish — academically, creatively and personally — within a community that knows them well and expects the very best of them.

We blend the warmth and rigour of a classical education with the energy and ambition of the wider world. Our pupils leave us not only with outstanding results, but with the character, confidence and conscience to lead good lives. Our motto guides everything we do: Curiosity, Character, Community.

A. Mercer
Dr. A. Mercer · Founding Head
