Experience Redwood

Your front door to our world

Choosing the right school is one of the most significant decisions a family can make. We believe you should be able to see, feel and truly explore our campus long before your first in-person visit — which is why we have built a fully immersive, 360° virtual tour of every corner of Redwood Science Academy.

From the light-filled corridors of our Lower School wing to the state-of-the-art science suites, from the warmth of our boarding houses to the sweep of our sports fields, every space tells the story of how seriously we take the environment in which our pupils learn and grow. Navigate at your own pace — there is a great deal to discover, and we think you will like what you find.

Highlights

What you will see

The tour covers six distinct zones of campus, each narrated by a current pupil or member of staff who knows it best. Toggle between spaces using the in-tour menu, or follow the guided path we recommend to first-time visitors.

Classrooms

Ergonomic, discussion-ready learning spaces fitted with interactive displays and flexible furniture — designed so that teachers can orchestrate any style of lesson, from Socratic seminar to hands-on enquiry project.

Science Labs

Purpose-built chemistry, biology and physics suites alongside a dedicated sixth-form research laboratory where DP and CP pupils pursue independent extended-essay investigations and Group 4 collaborative projects.

Library

A two-storey academic library housing over 18,000 print and digital resources, individual study carrels, a digital media suite and a dedicated reading lounge where younger pupils discover a lifelong love of books and ideas.

Boarding Houses

Three boarding houses — each with its own character and dedicated pastoral team — complete with common rooms, music practice spaces, supervised study halls and garden courtyards that become the beating heart of residential life.

Sports Complex

A 25-metre indoor pool, full-size sports hall, fitness suite, four floodlit tennis courts and an all-weather athletics track — home to 28 competitive school sport teams and our award-winning wellbeing programme.

The Grounds

Thirteen LEED Platinum-certified acres featuring woodland walks, kitchen gardens tended by our eco-committee, outdoor learning pavilions and broad social lawns that invite reflection, spirited play and quiet conversation alike.
