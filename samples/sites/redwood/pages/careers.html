Why Redwood

A place where teachers thrive

At Redwood Science Academy, our staff are the beating heart of everything we do. We believe that inspired teaching flows from inspired people — so we invest heavily in the professional development, wellbeing and career growth of every member of our community.

Whether you are an experienced IB educator or an exceptional graduate taking your first steps into the classroom, you will find colleagues who challenge you, pastoral structures that support you, and pupils who make the work profoundly worthwhile.

We are an IB Continuum World School with 32 nationalities on campus, a 1:8 teacher-to-pupil ratio, and a culture that places genuine care at its centre. If you share our conviction that education should change lives, we would love to hear from you.

What We Offer

Benefits of working at Redwood

Professional Development

A generous annual CPD budget, in-house IB training, attendance at international conferences and a structured mentoring programme for early-career educators.

Wellbeing & Support

A dedicated staff wellbeing co-ordinator, private healthcare, an employee assistance programme and a campus that genuinely looks after the people who work within it.

Global Community

Collaborate with educators from 28 countries, engage with a vibrant parent body spanning five continents, and build lasting professional relationships within the IB network.

Beautiful Campus

Work on a 13-acre LEED Platinum certified campus with purpose-built laboratories, arts studios, sports facilities and peaceful green spaces designed to inspire creativity.
