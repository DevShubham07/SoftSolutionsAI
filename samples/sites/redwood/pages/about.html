Our Story

Curiosity, Character, Community

Redwood Science Academy was founded on a simple conviction: that a great education joins intellectual rigour to genuine care. For more than two decades we have grown into a full IB Continuum World School, yet our purpose is unchanged — to know every child well and to expect the very best of them.

Rooted in a proud tradition of scientific and academic excellence and reimagined for a fast-changing world, we nurture intellect, integrity and independence in equal measure. Rigorous academics meet a vibrant campus life that builds resilience, friendship and a sense of belonging that lasts a lifetime. Our pupils leave us not only with outstanding results, but with the character, confidence and conscience to lead good lives.

What We Value

The principles that guide us

Curiosity

We teach children to ask better questions, to follow evidence wherever it leads, and to find joy in discovery across the sciences, the arts and beyond.

Character

Integrity, kindness and resilience are taught as deliberately as mathematics. We want graduates who lead good lives and lift others as they rise.

Community

A school is its people. Our house system, parent body and global alumni network turn a place of learning into a family that endures for life.

A heritage, carried forward

From our earliest days as a small science school, we have believed that the best learning happens when high expectations are wrapped in real warmth. That belief shaped our classrooms, our pastoral care and the way we welcome new families.

Today, as an IB Continuum World School, we hold accreditations and partnerships with the IB, Cambridge International, COBIS and Round Square — but the measure we care about most is simpler: do our children flourish? On every visit, we hope you will see that they do.
