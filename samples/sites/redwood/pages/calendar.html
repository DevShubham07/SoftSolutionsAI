2026–27 Academic Year

Term Dates

Our academic year is organised into three terms, each with its own rhythm of learning, pastoral care, co-curricular activity and celebration. We recommend that families plan all travel during confirmed school holidays to avoid any disruption to learning.

Autumn Term: Wednesday 2 September 2026 – Friday 18 December 2026
Half-term holiday: 26 October – 30 October 2026

Spring Term: Wednesday 6 January 2027 – Friday 26 March 2027
Half-term holiday: 15 February – 19 February 2027

Summer Term: Tuesday 20 April 2027 – Friday 2 July 2027
Half-term holiday: 31 May – 4 June 2027

INSET days for staff are the first two days of the Autumn and Spring terms. Pupils return the following day. All dates are subject to confirmation — please check the parent portal for the most current version.
